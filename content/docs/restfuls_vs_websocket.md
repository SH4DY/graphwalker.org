# What's the differenc running GraphWalker as a Restful vs. WebSocket service?

**Restful**
 * Synchronous
 * Simple to imlement a client
 * Can only serve 1 client

**WebSocket**
 * Asyncronous
 * More complex to imlement a client
 * Can serve several clients at the same time
